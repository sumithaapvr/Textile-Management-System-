Our Values

     At Textile Central, we are driven by the need to be environmentally sustainable, to be socially conscious and to empower our primarily female workforce.

     We are proud to operate in the labour-intensive textiles industry.We have the opportunity to create thousands of jobs for Indians at the bottom of the pyramid.

     These women and men are able to transition from the unorganised sector to corporate factory work, where they enjoy benefits such as excellent health insurance and free college-level courses.

     Thanks to our own wind mills and solar panels, we are able to sustainably power the majority of our operations through renewable energy.”

– Late Manikam Ramaswami (Ex- Chairman & MD, Textile Central)
